# Suleyman (2023) — The Coming Wave

**Full citation:** Suleyman, M., with Bhaskar, M. (2023). *The Coming Wave: Technology, Power, and the Twenty-First Century's Greatest Dilemma.* Crown.
**Type:** Trade book / policy argument
**Author:** Mustafa Suleyman (co-founder of DeepMind, CEO of Microsoft AI)

## Main Argument
AI and synthetic biology represent an unprecedented "coming wave" of technology that will transform society. Suleyman argues that the power and proliferation of these technologies is unstoppable, and that the central challenge is "containment" — governing technology without stifling its benefits.

## Key Themes Relevant to Paper
- Highly optimistic about AI's potential to solve major human problems, including health and education
- Argues AI is rapidly becoming cheap and widely accessible
- Discusses AI's potential to provide personalized tutoring, medical advice at scale
- Influential in popular discourse about AI's transformative potential

## Relevance to Paper
- Cited in the draft as an example of the "direct provision" modality gaining prominence in public discourse
- Represents the optimistic end of the spectrum that the paper's title ("Hope or Hype") is interrogating
- Useful contrast with rigorous empirical evidence

## Limitations / Caveats
- Trade book, not peer-reviewed
- Suleyman has obvious commercial interests in AI hype
- Strong on possibilities; does not engage with LMIC-specific enabling factor constraints
